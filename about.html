About Us



Welcome to our wine classification app!

We are a collection of wine enthusiasts who have come together to create a fun and interactive way to explore the world of wine.

This app is designed to predict the variety of wine based on its alcohol content. The app uses a machine learning model trained on a dataset of wine samples to make predictions. To get started, simply enter the alcohol content of the wine you would like to classify and click the "Predict" button. The app will then display the predicted wine variety based on the input provided. We hope you enjoy using our app and look for more wine applications from us in the future
